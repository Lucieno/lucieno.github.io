About Me

I’m a Ph.D. student in Computer Science at Georgia Tech and a 2023 Croucher Scholar. I work on privacy-enhancing technologies, focusing on multi-party computation with applications to privacy-preserving machine learning and zero-knowledge proofs. I also study blockchain and cryptocurrency systems to improve efficiency, privacy, and usability.

My current advisor is Prof. Vladimir Kolesnikov. My M.Phil. research at the Chinese University of Hong Kong was supervised by Prof. Sherman S. M. Chow.
